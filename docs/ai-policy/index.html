Policy on use of AI in my blog

I write about AI, so it seems valid to ask: how much AI do I use for writing my blog posts? Short answer: minimally.

The purpose of this blog is as much to help me think clearer as it is to publish content. Writing is an important part of my thinking process. As such, using AI to do the writing on my blog would be counter-productive. For this blog, I use AI for proofreading but not text generation. Further, some illustrations are generated with AI, I declare this use in-place.

This policy was inspired by Simon Willison’s version.

Policy in place since 2026, though I didn’t use much AI for writing before either.
